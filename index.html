About Me

I'm Tim, a full stack developer with experience creating sophisticated web applications using JavaScript and modern JavaScript frameworks. I currently tutor new developers around the country, and work as a freelancer developing client facing websites. I honed my skills at Rutgers University, where I completed the full stack development program. During my time at Rutgers, I had the pleasure to work with a group of the finest students and instructors on the planet.

In my spare time I enjoy attending JavaScript and modern technology meetups in Philadelphia, as well as completing projects for local non-profits. As I continue to grow as a developer, I hope to assist non-profit organizations better reach their consumer-base, and build dynamic tools to improve efficiency.
